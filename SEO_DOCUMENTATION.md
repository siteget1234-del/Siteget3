## Google Indexing Checklist

### To Get Indexed on Google:

1. **Submit Sitemap to Google Search Console**
   - Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Add your property (domain)
   - Submit sitemap: `https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`

2. **Request Indexing**
   - In Google Search Console, use "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter each page URL
   - Click "Request Indexing"

3. **Build Backlinks**
   - Submit to local business directories
   - Create social media profiles
   - Share on relevant forums and communities

4. **Create Google My Business**
   - Set up a Google My Business profile
   - Add business details, location, contact info
   - Link to your website

5. **Regular Content Updates**
   - Add blog posts or news updates
   - Keep content fresh and relevant
   - Update sitemap dates
